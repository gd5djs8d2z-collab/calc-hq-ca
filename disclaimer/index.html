Terms & Disclaimer

The short version: the numbers here are careful estimates to help you understand your situation, not a substitute for the CRA, Revenu Québec, or professional advice. Here's exactly what that means.

Estimates, for the 2026 tax year

Every calculator on calc-hq.ca produces an estimate for the 2026 tax year, using published federal, provincial, and territorial figures. It models a typical case — employment or self-employment income, standard credits and contributions. It does not know your full picture: other income, deductions, credits you qualify for, or anything specific to your circumstances. Treat the result as a well-sourced ballpark, not a final number.

Not tax, legal, or financial advice

calc-hq.ca is for general information only. Nothing on this site is tax, legal, accounting, or financial advice, and using a calculator doesn't create any advisor relationship. For advice about your own situation, speak to a qualified professional.

Verify with the authorities before you act

Before you make a decision or file anything, confirm the figures against the bodies that actually administer them:

  • the Canada Revenue Agency (canada.ca) for federal tax, CPP, EI, and benefits;
  • Revenu Québec (revenuquebec.ca) if you live or work in Quebec;
  • Service Canada for EI, CPP, and related benefit claims.

Where an official figure and a number on this site ever disagree, the official source is right.

No liability for decisions made from the output

You use these calculators at your own discretion. To the fullest extent permitted by law, calc-hq.ca is not liable for any loss or consequence arising from decisions or actions taken based on a calculator result or anything else on the site. The tools are provided "as is," without warranties of accuracy or fitness for a particular purpose.

How the numbers are kept accurate

None of the above means the figures are casual — the opposite. Every tax constant on this site is verified against the primary government source that publishes it and re-audited on a maintenance schedule, so the rates and thresholds stay current rather than drifting year to year. You can read more about that standard on the about page. The disclaimer exists because even accurate figures can't account for your specific situation — not because the numbers are loose.

Your inputs stay in your browser

The amounts you enter — salary, income, family details — are processed entirely in your browser to produce a result. They are not sent to us, stored, or shared, and there are no accounts to create. For the full detail on data and cookies, see the privacy policy.

Questions

If something looks wrong or you have a question about these terms, get in touch through the contact page. Corrections are genuinely welcome — accuracy is the point of the site.
