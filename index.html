Why one hub instead of ten single-page tools

Most Canadian tax calculators answer one narrow question and stop. This site groups the tools that belong together — everything about your paycheque in one place, everything about government benefits in another — so you can move from "what's my net pay" to "what will this bonus cost me" without starting over on a new site.

Every calculator here pulls from a single 2026 rates file that a human checks against the CRA's own tables before it goes live. When the brackets change each January, they change in one place, so the numbers you see stay current instead of drifting a year behind.
